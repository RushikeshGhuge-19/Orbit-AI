# ORBIT AI

### Enterprise Multi-Agent Intelligence Platform

> **An enterprise platform for building, orchestrating, and operating autonomous AI agents capable of securely executing business workflows across organizational systems.**

---

# Executive Summary

ORBIT AI is an enterprise-grade AI platform designed to enable organizations to deploy intelligent software agents capable of reasoning, planning, collaborating, and executing business workflows across enterprise applications.

Unlike traditional conversational assistants, ORBIT AI functions as an execution platform where autonomous AI agents securely interact with organizational knowledge, enterprise software, databases, APIs, and business processes while maintaining enterprise-grade governance, observability, reliability, and security.

The platform provides a unified runtime for intelligent agents by combining Large Language Models (LLMs), enterprise knowledge retrieval, workflow orchestration, tool execution, memory management, evaluation infrastructure, and enterprise integrations into a single operational environment.

---

# Business Problem

Modern enterprises operate across dozens of disconnected software platforms including:

- Email Systems
- Document Repositories
- Project Management Platforms
- Internal Databases
- Collaboration Tools
- Knowledge Bases
- Cloud Applications
- Custom Enterprise Services

Employees routinely spend a significant portion of their time:

- Searching for information
- Switching between multiple applications
- Executing repetitive operational tasks
- Coordinating manual workflows
- Copying data across systems

Although Large Language Models have introduced conversational AI assistants, most existing solutions primarily generate responses rather than executing meaningful work inside enterprise environments.

Organizations require AI systems capable of:

- Understanding organizational context
- Accessing enterprise knowledge securely
- Reasoning over complex business tasks
- Executing multi-step workflows
- Interacting with enterprise applications
- Operating under organizational governance
- Providing complete auditability and transparency

# Vision

To build a secure, scalable, production-ready AI platform that enables organizations to deploy autonomous digital workers capable of augmenting knowledge-intensive business operations.

ORBIT AI aims to become the intelligent execution layer connecting employees, enterprise knowledge, business applications, and AI agents into a unified operational ecosystem.

1. Product Requirements
2. Software Requirements Specification
3. High-Level Design
4. Low-Level Design
5. API Contracts
6. Database Design
7. Security Review
8. Implementation
9. Testing
10. Documentation

This ensures maintainability, scalability, and production readiness throughout the software development lifecycle.
